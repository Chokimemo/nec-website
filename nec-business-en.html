BUSINESS

CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

Construction Manager (CM) is a person responsible for the construction works including inspecting, approving, coordinating and certifying the works, materials, safety and construction methods of all subcontractors, builders and suppliers. Being a CM holds vital responsibility for which NEC will appoint only senior and dedicated professionals with broad-based knowledge and interest in construction related, especially for Substation Construction Management.

NEC’s Construction Management Services

Site physical analysis, accessibility studies, neighboring sensitivities

Advising on the construction feasibility, interfaces, and design schedule

Pre-Qualification of subcontractors

Construction methods advisory

Advising on construction schedule, and safety concerns for subcontractors tendering

Defining key performance indicators for subcontractors

Advising on the need for mock-ups, samples, third party testing, inspections, and design approvals

Advising on to construction costs, construction schedule with milestones, phasing, major deliveries and other site parameters.

Arranging for site facilities, fences, hoardings, roads and walkways, drainage, power, communications, IT, water supply and temporary services

Arranging site Inspection, testing, daily logs, monthly reports, Request for Information (RFI) from designers, site communications (SI’s, NCR’s, MOM, RFA’s, Visitors, etc.), safety meetings, subcontractor meetings, variations orders, and material substitutions.

Inspecting subcontractors works, issuing instructions and certificates

Testing and Commissioning

Collecting as-built drawings and manuals of pre and post construction phase
