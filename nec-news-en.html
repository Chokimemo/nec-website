Final Delivery for PEA 115/22kV Sophisai Substation

19 May 2022

The signing ceremony of the contract for the construction of Substation Udon5 project

On 26 July 2022, The Provincial Electricity Authority (PEA) and AA CONSORTIUM (ANSI and AIT Company) jointly signed a contract for the construction of a 115/22kV power station in Udon Thani 5 on the National Highway No. 2410 line around Udon Thani. With Mr. Praphan Sinuan, Deputy Governor of Engineering Provincial Electricity Authority and Mr. Apipol Chananugool, representative from AA CONSORTIUM (ANSI and AIT) jointly signed the contract. The project has a total duration of 510 days with a total project value of 228 million baht. To improve the quality of service to power users, ANSI and AIT will strictly and efficiently comply with the contract in accordance with the employer's policies and plans.
