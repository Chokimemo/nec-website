ABOUT NEC

History

At first, NEC was established in 2005 by Mr. Supakit Jetiyawan to conduct marketing transactions with partners and customers for flexibility in management.

When it opened a while ago. Group of customers investing and accepting foreign work. Lack of a project management team in terms of turnkey and EPCF construction work, there is a need for a professional management team. At that time company had a team of experienced management in various fields, such as industrial plant specialists, and the construction of power plants with stations and transmission lines in Thailand and abroad. include the Middle East, such as the petroleum and petrochemical Industries.

To support customers to need more investment in Thailand and surrounding countries such as Laos, Cambodia, and Myanmar. NEC adjusts the work plan by focusing on the consultant, project management, and construction management in each project to achieve the goals of the project owners, both public and private sectors including a comprehensive feasibility study.
