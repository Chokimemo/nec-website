About us

New Electric Company Limited. was established in 2005 with registered capital of 1,000,000 baht. by Mr. Supakit Jetiyawan for marketing engineering. After that 1 year, New Electric changed its marketing business. Come to be the management of various construction projects. Both Thai and International until now.

Currently, New Electric Company Limited has a management team in various fields with expertise. And more than 20 years of experience, ready to provide services in project management or consulting and feasibility analysis.
